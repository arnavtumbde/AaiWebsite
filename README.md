> *“Today, I am the author of my own peace. This is my Astitva.”*

A custom-built, high-performance editorial website and headless CMS blogging platform created for **Sonali Tumbde** — a Marathi teacher, published author, and survivor. This platform was built to provide her with a digital identity (“Astitva”) and a seamless writing experience.

---

# 📖 The Story Behind the Code

This is not just a blog platform — it is a digital monument to resilience.

My mother, Sonali, has 22 years of teaching experience and is the published author of *“Zindagi in Short”*. For years, her voice belonged to everyone else. As her son and a Computer Science student, I built this platform to give her a premium, distraction-free space to reclaim her narrative and share her writing with the world.

---

# 🚀 Tech Stack & Architecture

This project follows a modern **Jamstack architecture** to achieve high performance, scalability, SEO optimization, and a smooth authoring workflow.

## Frontend
- **Astro** — Static Site Generation for ultra-fast performance and SEO
- **Tailwind CSS** — Utility-first styling system
- **Framer Motion** — Smooth animations and transitions

## Backend / CMS
- **Sanity.io** — Headless CMS for content management
- **Resend API** — Automated newsletter email delivery

## Hosting & Deployment
- **Vercel** — Frontend hosting and deployment
- **Sanity Studio** — Content management dashboard
